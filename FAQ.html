FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. What is Bucketlistly?

Bucketlistly is a travel community and blog that provides inspiration, travel guides, and resources to help you create and accomplish your bucket list goals. We are a platform for sharing and discovering unique travel experiences from around the globe.

2. Who is Bucketlistly for?

Bucketlistly is for anyone with a passion for travel and a desire to live life to the fullest. Whether you're a seasoned backpacker or just starting to dream about your first big trip, you'll find a supportive community and a wealth of information to help you on your journey.

3. How can I get involved?

You can join our community by signing up with your Facebook account. This will allow you to create your own bucket lists, share your travel stories, and connect with other like-minded travelers. You can also subscribe to our newsletter to get the latest travel guides, itineraries, and stories delivered to your inbox.

4. Do you accept guest posts?

At this time, we do not accept guest posts unless you have a personal connection with us. We receive a high volume of inquiries and are focused on creating our own unique content.
